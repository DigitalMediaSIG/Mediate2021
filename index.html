MEDIATE

ICWSM-2021 WORKSHOP

Overview

The second Mediate workshop will be held virtually on June 7th, 2021, as part of the International AAAI Conference on Web and Social Media (ICWSM). The main goal of the workshop was to bring together media practitioners and technologists to discuss new opportunities and obstacles that arise in the modern era of information diffusion. This year's theme is Misinformation: automation, uptake, and digital governance.

Call for Papers

Misinformation is regarded as a problem that has reached new heights in the current information sphere, partly due to the ease, the diversity of mediums, the reach, and the consequences of information dissemination.

MEDIATE's topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

  • Automated methods against misinformation. In this workshop we would like to discuss the state-of-the-art in automated and human-in-the-loop methods aiming to assist in the information verification process, such as rumour detection, verification, fact-checking and stance classification. We welcome submissions proposing novel methodologies, describing their advantages and also challenges in creating accurate systems that would be adopted in practice by journalists and the public.
  • Uptake of automation. In the workshop we would like to discuss which automated solutions have been successfully adopted (e.g., identifying trending news/events, clustering relevant posts together),the challenges in the adoption of solutions addressing misinformation directly, and proposed (partial) solutions to said challenges (e.g., interpretable models). This will inform future lines of research, and encourage cross-domain collaborations with the potential for real-world impact.
  • Ethics, regulation and governance We would like to discuss the following questions. What types of interventions are the most effective to prevent the spread of false claims? What are the ethical boundaries of automated content moderation? How can one implement public and private governance on automated content moderation? What are the limits of automated information regulation? Interdisciplinary discussions around digital governance and democracy are of particular interest.
Submission instructions

Submissions must be anonymized and up to 10 pages long. Submissions must adhere to the AAAI format and be self-contained, in English and in PDF format.

The submission website is https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=mediate2021.
